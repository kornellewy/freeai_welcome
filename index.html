FreeAi

Platform to fast, free, knowledge free building, storing and deploing ai models also gives you thousands of models and datasets for free.

Get Started

End to end easy to use toolkit

Freeai is a platform that allows you to build datasets from your photos or photos from public and open datasets, and then train models on them. Then it enables their rigorous and very thorough testing. Finally, it allows you to download them with a configuration file that clearly explains how to use the model. You can also use the free model library where there are thousands of free models to download and ready to use with clear and often written examples.

Models

Freeai let you train ai models in domains like vision, nlp, reinforcement learning, time series and all of that completely free. All generated models comes with configuration file with examples of usage and explanation of work.

Datasets

Freeai have library of thousands of datasets and also provide you with tool to do your own dataset from openimages.

Suport

Freeai have forum where you can post your problems with models and also post ideas to improve platform.
